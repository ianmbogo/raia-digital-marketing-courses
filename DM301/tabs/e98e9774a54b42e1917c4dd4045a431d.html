As this course focuses on what a student needs, this page is specially made for you, Instructor. You are the only one with access to it and can see its content because we want to share some information with you.  

First, we expect this course will help you and your students on the first steps of using this platform and all the exciting educational opportunities this can mean. Feel free to modify or add any content to this course to make it more suitable for your students.

As an extra tool, here are two email templates you can use in any course to set up the messages your class will receive as welcome and farewell and the instructions on how to do it.

How to set up an email for a course:

Step 1:

From the live version of your course, click the Instructor tab on the Navigation bar.

Step 2:

In the Instructor panel, go to the Email tab, where you will find all the options to set up emails for your class.

Emails Template

Welcome Email Template:

       
								
Subject: Welcome to %%COURSE_DISPLAY_NAME%% - Get Ready to Learn!
           
Dear %%USER_FULLNAME%%,

Welcome to %%COURSE_DISPLAY_NAME%%. We are glad to start this course with you, which we believe will expand your horizons and equip you with valuable tools and insights that you can apply in your personal and professional life. 

You can access all course materials directly from the course platform, including lectures, readings, assignments, and quizzes. Log in using your credentials and navigate to the course page. Please take some time to go to the Home page and get familiar with course policies and philosophy.

I will be your course lead, and I hope you all have a great time learning! It may be challenging or frustrating, but it will be rewarding, and you will learn a ton.

On behalf of the staff, welcome, good luck, and have fun!

Farewell Email Template:

       
								
Subject: Final Remarks %%COURSE_DISPLAY_NAME%%

Dear %%USER_FULLNAME%%,

As we near the end of %%COURSE_DISPLAY_NAME%%, we want to take a moment to congratulate you on your dedication and hard work throughout this learning journey. Your commitment to expanding your knowledge and skills has been truly commendable.

We hope you have found the course content engaging and valuable and are leaving with a deeper understanding of course subjects. Completing a course is a significant achievement, and we are proud to have had you as a part of our learning community.

* If you qualify for a certificate (overall score to pass or higher), the
  dashboard will include a link to your certificate soon.
  While you may see the link in a few days, it can take up to two weeks for
  the platform to generate all of the course certificates.

* As an enrolled student, you can access the lecture videos 
  after the course ends. Assessments will remain, but you can no longer submit answers to any problem sets or exams with due dates.

* The discussions close on %%COURSE_END_DATE%%. You will not
  be able to add to the discussions after that time, but you will be able to
  continue viewing all of the conversations that took place during the
  course.

Once again, congratulations on your accomplishment, and we wish you all the best in your future endeavors. Keep learning, exploring, and growing.
