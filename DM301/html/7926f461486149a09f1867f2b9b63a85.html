Another vital part of the platform is the navigation bar at the top of the page. Here, you will find the essential options to navigate through your course, like the Course, Progress, Discussion, and other tabs that might be or not depending on the course.

  • Course: Clicking on this tab will take you to the course’s catalog and outline.
  • Progress: This tab will direct you to a page where you will see your progress on the course and if you are approving it.
  • Dates: Here you will find the most important dates of the course, like exams and closing dates.
  • Discussion: This tab will take you to the discussion forums, where you can communicate with fellow students on topics and projects and interact with the course staff.
  • Other tabs could be added depending on the course and the professor.
