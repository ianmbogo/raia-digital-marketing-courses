Professors who create courses on the platform can implement highly interactive experiences that allow you to experiment using online web applications as a student. These labs are customized to each class and subject area. We have collected examples of the more popular lab environments to experience and play with, like:

  • Mathematical expressions.
  • Chemical equations.
  • Electrical circuits.

Please be patient with yourself as you look around at these lab environments. You don't have to answer all the questions, but you get a good idea of how the labs and demos work.
