Often, you will have a limited number of times to check your answer. 

When a limited number of checks is available on a question, the Save button will appear on the right side. This will not check your answer or use up any of your submissions for the question. Instead, saving a response will keep the solution that you saved and present it back to you when you look at the question again.

Please note: to get a grade on your answer, click Submit to send your reply to the grader. As always, only your last checked answer counts for your grade. You will see the counter on the number of used and available submissions below the question.

Try entering an answer and saving it. You can also experiment by giving a wrong answer on purpose to see how the system behaves.
