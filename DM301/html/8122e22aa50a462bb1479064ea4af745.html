As we told you previously, at some point in your courses, you may encounter an essay writing exercise where you will have to respond in writing to a specific topic that will appear in the prompt of this section. 

You, your classmates, the staff-course, or all of them can review these essays, so you may also have to check your classmates' texts. Your professor will create a rubric to help the class review the papers.
