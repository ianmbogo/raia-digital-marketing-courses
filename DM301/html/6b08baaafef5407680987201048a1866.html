The platform system checks your answers to most questions and gives you immediate feedback. When you click Submit for a problem, it is automatically submitted for grading and will let you know if the answer is correct. But what if your answer is wrong? Then you will have two options. You might have another chance to answer, or you can find the option to check the correct answer. How?

You will find a Show Answer button on the right side of some questions that will give you an explanation in case you don't know the correct answer. Usually, this option will be available with questions with specific chances to answer.
