You will find the discussion forum of each course at the top of the course page. You also might come across a subset of the discussion forum inside one page, as you see below, where you can talk with fellow students about the class in context. So go ahead and be social! Make your first post in this demo course.

Keep an eye out for posts with a green check mark. The green check means a staff member or forum moderator has recognized the post as great content. You can also actively upvote a post. Others can search on user “upvoted” posts. They tend to be very helpful. 

How to add a post:

1. Click the Add a Post button on the right corner of the discussion forum below.  

2. Select the type of post that you want to add. It could be a Question or a Discussion.

3. Add the title of your post, like the name of the topic you want to discuss or something that presents it.

4. Write your message. Remember that no character limitations exist, so you can write as much as you need. In this section, you will have several options to modify the typography and content, such as hyperlinks, bold, and italics.

5. You can also choose if you want to have a follow-up of that publication or if you want to publish anonymously. To use either of these two options, check the box on the left side of each of them.

6. To finish, click the Submit button, and your comment will be published in the course forum.
