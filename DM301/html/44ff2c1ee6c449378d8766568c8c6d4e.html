To start, you must understand how the content will be organized on the platform. Each course is divided into sections, subsections, and units.

  • The Sections are the chapters of the course. For example, this course has five sections. Each section will be divided into one or more subsections.
  • The Subsections are like the subtitles of each chapter and are made up of units.
  • Each Unit is a content page with text, videos, forums, and different activities. For example, this page is a unit that is part of the first subsection of the first section of this course.
